Men Haircuts Women Haircuts

Men Haircut

Quiff

A quiff is a hairstyle with voluminous, styled-up front hair, often paired with short sides for contrast.

French Crop

The French crop is a short hairstyle with a cropped fringe, tapered sides, and slightly textured hair on top.

Slick Back

The slicked-back hairstyle features smooth, combed-back hair, typically styled with gel or pomade for a polished, sleek look.

Messy Hair

A messy haircut features tousled, textured hair with a natural, undone look, often styled with a light product.

Comb Over

A comb-over haircut involves neatly sweeping hair to one side, often paired with short sides for a clean, polished look.

Crew Cut

A crew cut is a short, uniform hairstyle with tapered sides and slightly longer hair on top for a clean look.

Faux Hawk

A faux hawk features short sides with longer, spiked hair in the center, mimicking a mohawk without full shaving.

Buzz Cut

A buzz cut is an ultra-short, evenly clipped hairstyle, offering a low-maintenance, clean, and military-inspired look.

High & Tight

A high and tight haircut features very short or shaved sides with a slightly longer strip of hair on top for a sharp look.

Curtains

A curtains haircut features long, parted hair framing the face, often with a middle or off-center part for a retro look.

Textured Fringe

A textured fringe combines a parted, face-framing style with added layers and texture for a modern, voluminous look.

Wolfcut

A wolf cut features choppy layers, voluminous texture, and a mix of shag and mullet styles for a bold, trendy look.

Women Haircuts

Side Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are diagonally styled fringe, softly framing the face, adding elegance and versatility to various hair lengths and textures.

Full Bangs

Full bangs are a straight-across fringe that covers the forehead, creating a bold, youthful, and stylish look for various hairstyles.

Face-Framing Waves

Face-framing waves are soft, flowing curls that highlight facial features, adding volume, movement, and a flattering, effortless look.

3 Layers Cut

A layered haircut features varying hair lengths, adding volume, movement, and texture for a lightweight, flattering, and versatile look.

Curly Bangs

Curly bangs feature soft, bouncy curls framing the forehead, adding a playful, textured element to any curly or wavy hairstyle.

Curtained Collarbone Cut

The curtained collarbone cut features layered hair parted at the center, with length reaching the collarbone for a soft, layered look.

Wavy Chop

A wavy chop features textured, choppy layers with natural waves, creating a relaxed, effortless look with movement and volume.

The Shullet

The shullet is a mix of a shag and mullet, with layered, textured hair in the front and back.

Layers With Curtain Bangs

Layers with curtain bangs combine soft, face-framing bangs and layered hair, creating volume, texture, and a stylish, effortless look.

Bob With Bangs

A bob with bangs features a chin-length or shorter cut with straight-across or side-swept bangs for a bold, chic look.

Short Wispy Waves

Short wispy waves feature light, soft waves with a tousled, effortless look, creating texture and volume for a playful style.

Side Part With Texture

A side part with texture features a deep side part, with voluminous, textured hair for a relaxed, stylish appearance.
