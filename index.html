ABOUT US

SemaQ Barber is located near Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM) Cawangan Kelantan in Machang. Officially opened on October 29, 2023, by Professor Ts. Dr. Salmiah Kasolang, Assistant Vice-Chancellor of the Malaysian Academy of SME & Entrepreneurship Development (MASMED), SemaQ Barber is a collaborative initiative between MASMED UiTM Kelantan and the College and Non-Resident Management Unit.

Our mission is to provide convenient grooming services for both male and female students on campus while offering entrepreneurial opportunities for those interested in the barbering trade. By utilizing university facilities, we aim to motivate students to engage in healthful activities and foster a spirit of entrepreneurship.

SemaQ Barber currently employs 10 registered workers, including 6 male and 4 female students. We source all our supplies from Kota Bharu through a partnership with Firdaus Barber, an UiTM alumni in Machang. Despite our strategic goals, we face challenges such as co-worker availability and skill gaps, particularly during peak hours, which can lead to longer wait times for customers.

At SemaQ Barber, we are committed to enhancing campus life and supporting student entrepreneurship by providing essential services in a university environment. Join us in our journey to create a vibrant and supportive community at UiTM Kelantan.
